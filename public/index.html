Unbound Art

UnboundArt connects the artist directly to the art lover, by making unique original art available for rental. UnboundArt believes that everybody should be able to enjoy art, regardless of their budget, with easy access to a growing inventory. The artist benefits by avoiding the fees typically collected by agents, and galleries, and gets to manage her own inventory. UnboundArt is committed to making the world a beautiful place through wide access to art.
